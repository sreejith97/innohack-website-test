About Hackathon

Startup Mahostav is an international Indo-Germanic hackathon initiated by two esteemed institutions namely, SRH University Heidelberg and Amal Jyothi College of Engineering. The 48-hours long live event will be conducted in three gripping phases. Participants are admitted as a team, each consisting of four members.

01

Team

The teams will compete against each other and time to showcase the best solutions by daunting the programming skills and win huge prizes.
